Return Policy

Easy and Hassle-Free Returns

At Eshifumbi Furniture, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not fully happy with your order, our return policy is designed to make the process simple and convenient for you.

Return Eligibility

Products can be returned within 14 days of delivery under the following conditions:

  • The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as when you received it.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) must be provided.
  • Custom or personalized orders are not eligible for return unless faulty.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact our Customer Service team via the Contact Page or call us directly. Provide your order number and reason for the return. Our team will guide you through the process.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed back to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges and Damaged Items

If your item arrived damaged or faulty, please notify us immediately within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as per your preference.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about returns, please reach out to us on our Contact Page. Our dedicated team is here to help.
